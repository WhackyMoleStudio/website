Become the number one mole miner!

Mineral Madness is a multiplayer first-person game taking place in a procedurally generated, fully destructible cave system.

Play with friends!

Supports up to 8 players in fast paced, chaotic matches. Enjoyable with both friends and strangers!

Blow stuff up!

Who needs guns when you have bombs? Use them to blow up minerals, the environment, or even other moles!

Collect minerals!

Use your tools to collect minerals as fast as possible and become the number one mole miner!

Explore the caverns!

Navigate the foggy cave filled with minerals, plants and mushrooms, but beware of the local wildlife!
